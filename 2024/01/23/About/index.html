About Me

I am a second-year CS Ph.D. student at EECS of University of California Merced, supervised by Prof. Meng Tang.

Previously, I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering at University of Southern California, working with EESSC.
I received my B.E. degree in Digital Media Technology at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, supervised by Prof. Chenqiang Gao.

My research interests are computer vision and efficient deep learning. More specifically,

  • Generative Vision-Language Models
  • Efficient Deep Learning
  • Neural Architecture Search
  • Infrared Image Processing
  • Small Object Detection
