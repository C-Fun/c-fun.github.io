About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at EECS of University of California Merced, supervised by Prof. Meng Tang.

Previously, I obtained my M.S. degree in EE at University of Southern California, working with EESSC.
I received B.S. in Digital Media Technology at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, supervised by Prof. Chenqiang Gao.

My research interests are computer vision and efficient deep learning. More specifically,

  • Generative Vision-Language Models
  • Efficient Deep Learning
  • Neural Architecture Search
  • Infrared Image Processing
  • Small Object Detection

I am actively looking for Research Internship in Summer 2024.
